Waste Reduction Week:

  
  Waste Reduction Week engages and empowers Canadians to reduce, reuse and recycle waste. WRW is currently held in the third week of October each year.

Website: http://www.wrwcanada.com/
 

 

Recycling Council of Ontario

  
    The Recycling Council of Ontario (RCO) is a not-for-profit, membership-based organization involved in policy, education and project work around the issues of consumption, waste generation, reduction and diversion, and recycling.

Website: https://www.rco.on.ca/
   
 
 

Green Manitoba

  
    Green Manitoba, a special operating agency (SOA) of the Manitoba Government,serves as your access point to acquire information about provincial sustainability issues. The Agency delivers programs and services related to waste reduction, climate change action, water conservation, sustainable transportation and education for sustainability on behalf of government departments and various other clients.

Website: http://greenmanitoba.ca/
