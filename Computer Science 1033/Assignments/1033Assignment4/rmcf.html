Di Maria became an official Real Madrid player on 28 June 2010, when Real Madrid posted on their website that they had agreed to terms with Benfica for the player's transfer. He then signed a five-year €25 million contract.

Di Maria made his debut on the fourth of August 2010 in a friendly match against Club América, in which Real Madrid won 3–2. He didn't wait too long to get his first goal in the uniform scoring a stunner againts Hércules CF in a friendly game On 22 August.

The league debut did not go so well for Di Maria when Real Madrid tied 0-0 against RCD Mallorca on 29 August in the 2010 Liga BBVA oppenner. On 18th of September, Angel scored his first league goal and the winner for Real Madrid in their 2–1 win over Real Sociedad. Later on during the year Di Maria opened his scoring record in the UEFA Champions League (CL) and finished the season with 6 goals in CL, and 6 in the Liga BBVA.
