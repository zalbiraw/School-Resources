A Bushel of Light

It is 1910. Maggie and her identical twin, Thomasina, have been separated for six years--years during which Maggie has worked as a Barnardo Home Child at the Howards' farm, near Peterborough. Fourteen year old Maggie must do all of the never ending house work, help with the farm chores, and take care of four year old Lizzy. Now, however, she has begun to dream about Thomasina, and to consider running away. Where will Maggie's dreams take her?

Across the Steel River

Two boys find an old Indian war hero beaten almost to death in 1950's Saskatchewan. There is no investigation. The police call it an 'accident'. The one 'white' boy decides, along with his Indian friend, that the death should be looked in to. But who will do it? All the adults look at it from a distance and don't appear to care. Then there is the classroom bully who seems to get away with everything.

Bone Beds of the BadLands

Dylan Maples and his best friends have won first place in the National Science Fair competition. Their prize? A parent-free trip to the Badlands of Alberta, home of the ancient dinosaurs. All they have to do is put up with their science teacher and his wife as their chaperones. The trip might be cut short, however, when they find out that there's a manhunt on for an escaped killer. "The Reptile" was last seen headed for the perfect place to hide - the Badlands.

 

Bulley Boys

The year is 1812 and Tom Roberts wishes with all of his heart that he could fight alongside his father to drive the Americans from his home. Instead, he is left to work on the family farm with his mother. Fate steps in when Tom makes a simple visit to the general store and ends up helping to foil a robbery attempt by two Americans. The daring soldier whom he helps turns out to be James Fitzgibbon, leader of the "Bully Boys", a group of British army regulars. Before he has time to think, Tom Roberts' adventure with the Bully Boys has begun.

 

By the Standing Stone

When two cousins, Mack and Jamie are kidnapped from near their home along the Niagara River, their odyssey begins. Together they face winter in a harsh environment as they are dragged to the United States to be sold as slaves. Rescue comes from some unlikely sources as the two spirited youngsters struggle to be free and to return to their home.

Clara's War

Clara is a young Jewish girl who is deported to Terezin, a Jewish ghetto in Czechoslovakia, during World War II. Although the living conditions were as poor and restrictive as those found in Nazi concentration camps, the residents were able to maintain a strong cultural life. Clara makes many friends inside Terezin. A new friend, Jacob, takes Clara's brother under his wing. Together these young people face the constant challenges of life in a Jewish ghetto.

Dust

Some very unusual things have been happening in Horshoe, Saskatchewan. Seven-year-old Matthew has disappeared while walking into town to buy gum and only his older brother Robert seems to care. At first upset by their son's disappearance, Robert's parents soon forget Matthew, as they and the rest of the town are mesmerized by a stranger named Abram Harsich. It's the middle of the Depression and everyone seems convinced that Harsich's strange rain making machine is their only hope. He's convinced everyone that it will solve all their problems - only Robert seems aware that something much deeper and much darker is going on. Why are all the children disappearing? And why are all the adults acting like robots, using every spare minute to help build Harsich's device?

Stones

Garnet is a logical boy, with a scientific mind who has spent most of his school career on the outside of the "in group". He meets and is instantly attracted to Raphaella, an unusual, new girl who arrives in his English class. One night, in a snowstorm, Garnet stumbles onto strange apparitions at an abandoned African church . Together he and Raphaella uncover an awful secret, from their community's past that still reverberates in modern times. With the help of a century old diary and their own courage and honesty they set out to solve the mystery of a ghost.

Whisperings of Magic

This fantasy adventure reunites Catryn and Dahl after a three year separation. In that time, Dahl has become King of Taun bringing peace and prosperity to his kingdom. Having developed her powers of magic to become Seer of Taun, Catryn senses danger for her friend Dahl and his people. Together they embark on a quest to discover the evil that threatens their kingdom.

Who's Side Are You On?

Ron's life is in an uproar. His father has been even more critical than usual lately. Is it because of the bitter strike at the local mine where his dad and almost everyone else in town works? Or is it because his dad has to take a special trip to see a doctor? Ron needs to get his mind off girls and focus on finding out what's really happening to his family.
