H. Cross Company is a specialty metal rolling company fulfilling customers' needs for 65 years. We sell our products to a worldwide customer base. We manufacture with a wide range of metals including: Rhenium, Molybdenum, Tungsten, Tantalum, Precious Metals and many more including Customer Materials.
You may not know our name but you use our products, as we are a supplier to companies like General Electric & Ushio Lighting, L3, Boeing, CPI and Teledyne. Our products are used in lighting, electronic systems, medical applications, cathodes, contacts and many others. They can also be found in our nation's defense systems including the F-18, Patriot & Tow missile systems, radar systems and many more.
